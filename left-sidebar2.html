    District Merit Scholarship (DMS), Kerala

    Provider- Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala
    Eligibility- • Students pursuing studies at higher secondary, VHSC, ITI, or Polytechnic courses can apply.
    • They must have passed the SSLC examination conducted by the Board of Public Examination, Kerala State with an ‘A’ plus grade in all subjects.
    Award – INR 1,250 per annum
    Period* – Between October and December
    Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal.

    State Merit Scholarship, Kerala

    Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala
    Eligibility • Students pursuing studies in the first year of an undergraduate or postgraduate course in a government/aided college or university can apply.
    • The minimum percentage of marks to be obtained by them in the qualifying examination should be 50% or above.
    • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 1,00,000.
    Awards • For graduates – INR 1,250 per annum each to 300 students
    • For postgraduates – INR 1,500 per annum each to 150 students
    APPLICATION PERIOD* – Between October and December
    Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal.

    Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship (SJMS), Kerala

    Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala
    Eligibility – • Open for students who are in the first year of any undergraduate or postgraduate degree program.
    • They must belong to a BPL family.
    • The minimum percentage of marks to be obtained by them in the last qualifying examination is 50% or above.
    Awards – INR 10,000 per annum towards pursuing higher education
    APPLICATION PERIOD – Between October and December
    Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal

    Central Sector Scholarship (CSS), Kerala

    Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala
    Eligibility – • The students, who are above the 80th percentile of successful candidates in Class 12 from respective Board of Examination can apply for this scholarship.
    • They must be pursuing regular degree courses at the undergraduate or postgraduate level from institutions/colleges recognized by AICTE/UGC/MCI/DCI or any other regulatory authorities.
    • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 8,00,000 from all sources.
    Awards • For graduation level – INR 10,000 per annum for the first three years and INR 20,000 per annum for 4th and 5th year (for integrated/5-year courses)
    • For postgraduation level – INR 20,000 per annum
    APPLICATION PERIOD* – Between August and October
    Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
    *The application period mentioned above is tentative and may change at the discretion of the scholarship provider

This is the Minority based Scholarships

Criteria :

  • Minority Category: Belonging to a recognized minority community (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi).
  • Income Criteria: Families falling below a certain income threshold are usually eligible.
  • Academic Performance: Meeting minimum academic standards, like a specified percentage in the last qualifying examination.
  • Course of Study: Some scholarships might be specific to particular courses or fields of study.
  • Residence: Some scholarships may have region-specific or residency criteria.
  • Documentation: Providing proof of minority status, usually a certificate issued by a competent authority.
  • Government Schemes: Many scholarships are part of government schemes, so meeting their specific eligibility criteria is vital.
