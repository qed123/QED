    Post metric Scholarships sheme for minorities

    Provider : Central government
    Eligibility : Class 11 to Ph.D Students
    NSP Post Matric Scholarships Scheme for Minorities 2022-23 is an initiative for the Class 11 to Ph.D. students of minority communities. The scholarship is being offered by the Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India. The scholarship will be awarded for studies in India in a Government or private school including residential Government institutes and eligible private institutes that have been selected and notified by the respective State Government and Union Territory administration in a transparent manner.

    MCM scholarships for professional and technical courses

    Provider : Central government
    Eligibility : students pursuing professional and technical courses
    NSP Merit Cum Means Scholarship for Professional and Technical Courses CS (Minorities) 2022-23 is an initiative for minority community students who are pursuing technical or professional courses at the undergraduate or postgraduate level. The scholarship is being offered by the Ministry of Minority Affairs (MoMA), Government of India. The scholarship aims at providing suitable financial assistance to meritorious and underprivileged students belonging to minority communities to enable them to pursue technical and professional courses. The selected candidates will receive course fees and maintenance allowance.

    Central Sector Scheme of Scholarships for college and Univeristy students

    Provider : Central government
    Eligibility : students pursuing professional and technical courses
    NSP Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students 2023-24 is a scholarship program for meritorious students from economically weaker sections to pursue higher education in colleges and universities. The scholarship is offered by the Ministry of Education and is administered through the National Scholarship Portal (NSP). The main objective of this scheme is to provide financial assistance to meritorious students from poor families to meet a part of their day-to-day expenses while pursuing higher studies. The selected candidates will receive scholarship of up to INR 20,000 per annum

    Pst metric scholarshps for students with Disabilities

    Provider : Central government
    Eligibility : students pursuing professional and technical courses with disabilties
    NSP Post Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities 2023-24 is an initiative of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Government of India for physically disabled students from Class 11 to postgraduate level. The key objective of the scholarship is to support students with disabilities in their studies and prepare them to earn their livelihood and find a dignified place for themselves in society. The selected candidates will receive a maintenance allowance, book grant, reimbursement of compulsory non-refundable fees and disability allowance.

Common criteria :

  • Academic Achievement: Most scholarships require a certain GPA or academic achievement threshold.
  • Standardized Test Scores: Some scholarships may consider scores from standardized tests like SAT, ACT, or other relevant exams.
  • Field of Study: Specific scholarships might target students in particular B.Tech specializations or disciplines.
  • Financial Need: Certain scholarships are need-based and take into account the financial situation of the student or their family.
  • Leadership and Extracurricular Activities: Demonstrated leadership skills, involvement in clubs, sports, volunteer work, or other extracurricular activities could be considered.
  • Essays or Personal Statements: Many scholarship applications require essays or statements explaining your goals, aspirations, or reasons for applying.
  • Letters of Recommendation: Some scholarships may ask for letters of recommendation from teachers, mentors, or community leaders.
  • Diversity and Background: There might be scholarships specifically aimed at supporting students from diverse backgrounds, underrepresented communities, or certain demographics.
  • Research or Projects: If you've undertaken any significant research or projects in your field, it might strengthen your scholarship application.
  • Community Involvement: Involvement in community service or initiatives might be a criterion for some scholarships.
