      Blind/PH Scholarship, Kerala

      Blind/PH Scholarship, Kerala Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala Eligibility – • This scholarship is applicable to blind/physically handicapped/deaf students. • They must be pursuing their studies in government/aided Arts and Science Colleges, Music Colleges, and Higher Secondary/Vocational Higher Secondary schools. Awards • Blind students with annual family income below INR 2,50,000 will receive a fee waiver • Physically disabled students (hostellers) with family income below INR 4,50,000 will receive a waiver in hostel charges • Physically disabled students (day scholars) with family income below INR 4,50,000 will receive a waiver in boarding charges. Application Period* – Between October and December Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal.

      post metric sholarship for students with disabilities

      • The scheme has been launched by the Department of Disability Affairs during the Financial Year 2014-15. • The objective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to the parents of students with disabilities for studying in the post matric level. The parental income ceiling per annum is Rs 2.5 lakhs. • The financial assistance includes scholarship, book grant, escort/reader allowance, etc. Based on the type of course, a scholarship amount of Rs 900 - Rs 1600 per month for day scholars and Rs 550 - 750 per month for hostellers is provided. A tution fee ceiling of a maximum of Rs 1.50 lakh per annum and a book grant and disability allowance ranging from Rs 2000 - Rs 4000 per annum are also provided. • Number of scholarships to be granted every year is 17,000 for post matric level • Selection of the beneficiaries is on the basis of merit after the recommendation of the Governments of State or Union Territories. • For online application to the scheme

      Sanskrit Scholarship (SSE), Kerala

      Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala Eligibility – The scholarship is open for students pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate degree courses with Sanskrit as a subject. Awards • For undergraduate courses – 55 scholarships of INR 200 per month to each scholar • For postgraduate courses – 25 scholarships of INR 200 per month to each scholar Application Period* – Between October and December Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal Music Fine Arts Scholarship (MFAS), Kerala Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala Eligibility – The students studying in a Government Music College or Government Fine Arts College can apply for this scholarship. Awards – Financial assistance of up to INR 1,500 per annum Application Period* – Between October and December Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal. Hindi Scholarship (HS), Kerala Provider – Department of Collegiate Education, Government of Kerala Eligibility – Students pursuing an undergraduate or postgraduate course with Hindi as a subject can apply for this scholarship. Awards • For undergraduate students – 180 students will receive INR 500 per month • For postgraduate students – 59 students will receive INR 1,000 per month Application Period* – Between October and December Application Process – Applicants can apply online through the Department of Collegiate Education’s Online Scholarships Portal.
