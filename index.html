Financial Support: Scholarships remove financial barriers, enabling students from low-income backgrounds to access quality education, thereby promoting diversity and inclusivity.

    Equality and Inclusivity: Education promotes equal opportunities for all, irrespective of social or economic backgrounds. It reduces poverty and creates a more inclusive society by empowering marginalized groups.
